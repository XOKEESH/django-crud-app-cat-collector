About the ᓚᘏᗢ Collector

Welcome to Cat Collector, the purr-fect place to manage your feline family! This app is designed for cat enthusiasts who want to keep track of their beloved cats and all the special details that make them unique.

What can you do with Cat Collector?

  • Create and Manage Cats: Add all your cats to your collection, complete with names, breeds, ages, and unique traits.
  • Track Feedings: Log when and what your cats are fed to keep them happy and healthy.
  • Add Toys: Build a toy collection for each of your cats, because playtime is just as important as mealtime!

Why Cat Collector?

We believe every cat deserves love, care, and a touch of organization! Cat Collector makes it simple to keep track of your cats' daily routines and favorite things. Whether you're managing one cat or a clowder, this app is here to help you stay on top of it all.

"Because nine lives need a little organization."

Get started today and give your cats the attention they deserve. With Cat Collector, your feline friends will always have their needs met—right at your fingertips.
